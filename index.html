Graphic design is all about visual communication.

⌄

Designers use color, typography, and layout to convey messages effectively. Every design decision — from font size to color choice — influences how a viewer feels and what they understand.

Design is everywhere.

⌄

From logos and websites to advertisements and social media graphics, design shapes almost every visual experience in our daily lives. Learning design gives you a new way to see the world.

About

This website is dedicated to teaching the basics of graphic design and demonstrating parallax scrolling.

Graphic design is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas through visual content. It combines images, text, and layout techniques to create engaging designs used in branding, advertising, web design, and more. Popular tools include Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator, which allow designers to create both raster and vector graphics.

Typography is a key element of design.

⌄

Choosing the right font can completely change how a message is perceived. Serif fonts feel traditional and trustworthy, while sans-serif fonts feel modern and clean. Script fonts add elegance, while display fonts make a bold statement.

Color theory helps designers create impactful designs.

⌄

Understanding warm vs. cool tones, complementary colors, and color psychology allows designers to evoke specific emotions. Red signals urgency or passion, blue conveys calm and trust, and yellow radiates optimism.

Tutorials

Explore beginner-friendly tutorials to start your graphic design journey.

Create logos in Adobe Illustrator.

⌄

Start with basic shapes using the Shape Tool, combine them with the Pathfinder panel, then add typography. Use vector paths so your logo scales infinitely without losing quality. Export as SVG or PDF for professional use.

Edit photos in Adobe Photoshop.

⌄

Master layers and adjustment layers for non-destructive editing. Use the Pen Tool to make precise selections, blend modes to composite images, and smart filters to apply effects you can always tweak later.
