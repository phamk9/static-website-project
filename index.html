About

This website is dedicated to teaching the basics of graphic design and demonstrating parallax scrolling.

Graphic design is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas through visual content. It combines images, text, and layout techniques to create engaging designs used in branding, advertising, web design, and more. Popular tools include Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator, which allow designers to create both raster and vector graphics.

Typography is a key element of design. Choosing the right font can completely change how a message is perceived.

Color theory helps designers create visually appealing and emotionally impactful designs.
